## Margin Left Utility Class

### Class: `.margin-left-20px`

- **Description:** Adds a left margin of 20 pixels to an element.
- **Style:**
  - `margin-left: 20px;`

---

### Usage Example

#### Adding Left Margin to a Div

```html
<div class="margin-left-20">This div has a left margin of 20px.</div>
```
